About Me
👋 Who I am

Hi, I'm Jyoti! A supply chain professional based in Almere, Netherlands, with over 7 years of experience spanning reverse logistics, inventory management, business development, and ERP-driven operations across India and Europe.

I'm a true people person — I build strong working relationships naturally, and I believe great supply chain results happen through great teamwork.

I'm also deeply data-driven: I love working with data, finding patterns, and turning insights into operational improvements. Excel (Macro/VBA), SQL, Tableau, and Power BI are part of my everyday toolkit.

WMS System Transition · GXO Logistics (2022–2024)

At GXO Logistics (Schiphol-Rijk), I was part of a major Warehouse Management System transition — supporting data migration, process documentation, and training colleagues, while managing 5,000+ SKUs at 98.5% inventory accuracy.
