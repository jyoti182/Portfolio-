About Me
👋 Who I am

Hi, I'm Jyoti! A supply chain professional based in Almere, Netherlands, with over 7 years of experience spanning reverse logistics, inventory management, business development, and ERP-driven operations across India and Europe.

I'm a true people person — I build strong working relationships naturally, and I believe that great supply chain results happen through great teamwork. Whether it's coordinating with warehouse teams, finance, or senior stakeholders, I bring energy and clarity to every collaboration.

I'm also deeply data-driven: I love working with data, finding patterns, and turning insights into operational improvements. Tools like Excel (including Macro/VBA), SQL, Tableau, and Power BI are part of my everyday toolkit.

🔄 Key Projects

WMS System Transition · GXO Logistics (2022–2024)

At GXO Logistics (Schiphol-Rijk), I was part of a major Warehouse Management System (WMS) transition. I actively supported the team through the changeover — helping with data migration, process documentation, and training colleagues on the new system workflows. Managing 5,000+ SKUs during this live transition required both precision and adaptability, and we achieved 98.5% inventory accuracy throughout.

ERP Migration AFS → SAP S/4HANA · Nike (2025–Present)

At Nike, I am part of the team navigating a significant ERP transition from the legacy AFS (Apparel & Footwear Solution) platform to SAP S/4HANA. I support the transition by handling RSO processes in NikeFlow, validating data integrity during migration windows, coordinating with MSC, warehouse, and finance teams to ensure SLA compliance, and identifying process improvements that align with the new system's capabilities.

✅ Streamlining operations through process design and data analysis
✅ Leveraging tools: Excel, SQL, SAP, Tableau, Power BI
✅ Continuous improvement — always asking "how can we do this better?"
✅ Customer satisfaction and service excellence
✅ Sustainability in supply chain practice
